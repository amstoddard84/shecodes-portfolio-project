Hi, I'm Anne Marie.

A Content Developer, Strategist, & Storyteller based in Atlanta, GA.

I wear many hats: copywriter, content strategist, front end developer, storyteller, wife, mother, toddler and dog wrangler, hula hooper, music lover, bookworm, coffee addict, running enthusiast. I'm at my best when I'm working on creative projects that challenge my problem-solving skills and allow me to make a difference. I'm a passionate advocate for work/family life harmony and for supporting working parents. I enjoy spending time with my family and dog, experiencing live music, painting, camping, hiking, running 5K races, and watching hockey (Go Caps!) and football (Go Falcons and Dawgs!). For a closer look at my professional and creative endeavors, check out the For Work, For Practice, and For Play pages of this site.
