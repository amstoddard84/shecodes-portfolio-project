For Fun

Take a look at some of my passion projects.

Murder Mysteries

Under the name Anne Marie Stoddard, I'm a USA TODAY bestselling author of seven mystery novels and three short stories (and counting, of course!), spanning across four cozy mystery series. I love creating fictional worlds and developing multi-dimensional characters who work together to solve a good old fashioned whodunit.

Book Cover Design

In addition to writing novels, I've also designed the covers for five of my own stories from both my Amelia Grace and Darcy Harbinger mystery series. My creativity shines when I'm able to translate the concept of a story through visual design.
