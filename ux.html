Check out the case studies from my Google UX Certification coursework.

ShiftSwift Scheduling App

In the first phase of the Google UX certification course, I designed a work scheduling app that allows users to create/edit work calendars and send push notifications to scheduled workers. Through this project I learned the importantance of accessibility and devising the simplest possible user flow. Read the Full Case Study

Castle Rock Concert Ticketing Flow

My second project was to design a simple concert ticket ordering flow for a fictional concert venue. I was challenged to create a responsive design that could be optimized for both mobile and desktop screens. Read the Full Case Study

Hey Baby Parenting App

In my final project, I designed a parenting advice app for new parents. In my usability study, I learned that users prioritize accessibility as they're often juggling an infant in one hand and their mobile device in the other, so I incorporated large fonts, clickable icons, and a hands-free audio option to make content easier to consume.
