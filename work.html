For Work

See some of my most notable work from my career in content marketing, creation, & strategy.

Email Campaigns

I designed responsive email templates for several of Newsweek's email subscription offerings, including The Starting 5, Road to Recovery, and STEM Informer. In spring and summer of 2020, I provided content strategy and audience engagement consultation for these newsletters and helped increase The Starting 5's CTR by 9.52%

National Contests

While working for Engine Media (formerly Frankly Media & Triton Digital), I owned all aspects of monthly nationally-syndicated campaigns with an annual budget of $50,000. I planned, designed, implemented, and handled fulfillment for 87 sweepstakes featured on websites owned by CNN, Newsweek, Cumulus, iHeart, American General Media, Radio One, and many other US broadcasters.

Literary Promotions

As a freelance literary marketing assistant for Lucinda Literary, I worked with a roster of notable authors, including Richard Cohen, Ginny Gilder, Douglas Kennedy, Juliana Barbassa, Nina Darnton, and Anna Lefler. I optimized social media campaigns, designed email templates, and provided creative marketing consultation. A Twitter campaign I launched for Richard Cohen in 2016 increased his follower engagement by over 90%.

Technical Content Strategy

As the Content Developer and Technical Writer for NCR's Developer Experience Portal, I serve as a software evangelist, content strategist, and advocate for developers, customers, and partners in the hospitality, retail, and financial industries. In my first few months, I have launched NCR's Business Services Platform's new series of onboarding documentation and contributed to the development of content for college hackathon sites including UGA Hacks.
